    ハウルの動く城は、魔法使いのハウルと呪いで老婆になった少女ソフィーの冒険と恋を描いたアニメーション映画です。ソフィーはハウルの動く城に住み込み、掃除婦として彼と仲良くなりますが、戦争や魔法使いのサリマンや荒地の魔女などの脅威に直面します。ソフィーはハウルの心臓を守るために奮闘し、自分も呪いから解放されます。最後にはハウルと愛を確かめ合い、新しい城で幸せに暮らします。

    Defining the Unit Cell

    In GROMACS, both the dodecahedron and the octahedron are special cases of triclinic unit cells, which are the most general space-filling unit cells that comprise all possible space-filling shapes. These two shapes are closer to being a sphere than a cube is, and are therefore better suited to the study of an approximately spherical macromolecule in solution, since fewer solvent molecules are required to fill the box given a minimum distance between macromolecular images. This can save about 29% of CPU-time when simulating a spherical or flexible molecule in solvent. -------
